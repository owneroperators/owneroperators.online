What the house *doesn't* come with. Donnelly's small — load up on the Boise run and
haul it up. ([Packing](/retreat/packing/) is what you already own; this is the gap.)

> **Crew note:** Caleb's vegetarian (vegan when possible) — keep the haul stocked
> with veg/vegan mains, not just sides. Plan a few fully vegan-friendly meals.

## Kitchen / coffee

- [ ] **Keurig pods** — the house has the machine, not the pods. The eternal rental
      bait-and-switch. Grab a variety box, plus extra of whatever the crew actually drinks.

## Pantry / condiments

- [ ] **Hot sauce** — never trust a rental to have your heat. Bring your own.
- [ ] **Vegan mayo** (Vegenaise / Just Mayo) — also the base for potato salad, slaw, aioli

## Grill / cookout

House has a great gas range **and** a grill — real cooking's on the table.

- [ ] **Veggie dogs** — for Caleb, on purpose this time
- [ ] **Regular dogs + buns** — the good stuff for everyone else
- [ ] **Fixings** — mustard, ketchup, relish, onions, kraut
- [ ] **Burgers** + veggie / black-bean patties · buns
- [ ] **Propane** — the grill runs on propane. Check the tank, grab a spare

> **Sunday's the big cookout.** Make the Sunday grill the centerpiece of the week.

## Kid snacks

- [ ] Easy kid-friendly snacks + a fun dessert (s'mores fixings? the grill's right there)
- [ ] A couple of drink options the kids will actually like

## Something nicer (one proper dinner)

Don't do dogs all weekend — pick one night for a real meal. Vegan-friendly so the
whole table eats the same good thing.

- [ ] **A grilled main worth the name** — veggie skewers, stuffed portobellos, or grilled flatbreads (vegan cheese)
- [ ] **Real sides** — potato salad or slaw (that's the vegan mayo), grilled corn, a big salad
- [ ] **Good bread** + a dip (hummus / baba ganoush)
- [ ] **A proper dessert** (vegan-friendly)

### The pick: build-your-own grilled fajita bar

Low prep, feeds the crew, naturally vegan with an omnivore add-on. Slice peppers +
open jars; the grill does the rest. Everyone builds their own.

**Grill (veg base, in a basket — vegan):**

- [ ] 5 bell peppers (mixed) · 3 yellow onions
- [ ] 6–8 portobello caps, sliced (the "meaty" main) · 2 zucchini (optional)
- [ ] Olive oil + fajita/taco seasoning

**Omnivore add-on (grill separately):**

- [ ] ~2 lb chicken thighs or skirt steak — pre-sliced

**Carriers + filler:**

- [ ] Tortillas (flour + corn) · 2–3 cans black beans · rice pouches · tortilla chips ×2

**Toppings bar:**

- [ ] Guacamole · salsa ×2 · pico · limes ×4 · cilantro
- [ ] Slaw mix → quick crema slaw with the **vegan mayo** + lime
- [ ] Shredded cheese (vegan + regular) · sour cream (vegan + regular)

**Dessert (grill it):**

- [ ] Fresh pineapple, sliced — grill with brown sugar + cinnamon
- [ ] Vegan vanilla ice cream alongside
