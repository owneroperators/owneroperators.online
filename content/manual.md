We reject spectacle that doesn't risk sincerity. Nostalgia is a form of grief rebranded as comfort — recursion is a form of nostalgia. Authenticity is now performed so convincingly that even the algorithm believes it. Cynicism is fear in a bomber jacket that we refuse to wear. We do not play shows. We deploy payloads. Risk sincerity. Risk the flag.

Productivity ≠ Purpose. Never let perfection block transmission. A mistake, repeated with intention, becomes a hook. Make something honest before you make something good. Treat collaboration like communion, not content strategy. Dance anyway, especially if you're bad at it. Revert when you're done — that's the reward, not the bug.

Our revolution is infrastructural — not in bridges and pipelines, but in the circuits of care, curiosity, and shared snacks. Honesty without kindness burns bridges. Kindness without honesty builds cages. Truth is a place you can play from, like the sticky floor of a basement show. Load the room with something that survives rehearsal. Delight is resistance when the system only wants you scrolling.
