**June 16–23 · Donnelly, Idaho · a writing + recording retreat.** Band and family,
instruments and a mobile rig, a house with two living spaces near Lake Cascade. The
goal is keepers. This is the loose plan and the haul list — checked off as it loads.

## §0 — WHERE

Up ID-55 from Boise (~75 mi, ~1.5–2 hrs), then west across to the far shore of Lake
Cascade. The house sits on the **West Mountain** side — lake to the east, Tamarack
just south, Donnelly the nearest town, McCall ~15 min north for the real grocery /
pharmacy run.

{{< retreatmap >}}

## §1 — THE SHAPE OF THE WEEK

Heroes and a checklist, not an hour-by-hour cage. The week should breathe.

- **Tue 6/16 — Arrive.** Grocery run, check in after 4, claim the loft, stage gear in the garage, easy first dinner. Don't try to track tonight.
- **Wed 6/17 — Set up + first session.** Build the rig, get the room sounding right, cut something scrappy just to break the seal.
- **Thu 6/18 → Sun 6/21 — Work + play.** The meat: midday loud tracking, evening quiet work, lake / Tamarack / backcountry in between. Sauna nightly.
- **Mon 6/22 — Land the plane.** Comp and back up everything, last vocals and overdubs, a final field-recording pass. Start packing non-essentials.
- **Tue 6/23 — Out by 11.** Load, sweep for forgotten cables and drives, drive home.

## §3 — WHAT GOES IN THE TRUCK

### Clothing (mountain June — warm days, cold nights)

- [ ] Layers — the nights drop into the 40s–50s
- [ ] Rain shell (afternoon mountain storms)
- [ ] Swimsuits + towels (lake + sauna)
- [ ] Sturdy shoes for the backcountry
- [ ] Sun hat + sunglasses (high-elevation sun)

### Water + fun gear (Lake Cascade is right there)

- [ ] **Paddleboard** ×1 · **inflatable kayaks** (single + double) · **fly rod** ×1
- [ ] **Pump** for the inflatables (+ a hand-pump backup) — nobody's lung-inflating a kayak at 5,000 ft
- [ ] Paddles — SUP + kayak (1 + at least 2) · SUP leash · fins / fin needles
- [ ] **PFDs for everyone on the water** — Idaho law, one per person aboard
- [ ] **Repair kit** + patch glue — one thorn ends the trip otherwise
- [ ] Dry bag for phones/keys · a towel pile
- [ ] **Idaho fishing license** — grab it online before you go · flies / tippet / nippers / net

> Buttercup Boat Launch is a short walk from the house — park at the house and walk gear down if the ramp lot is packed.

### Kids

- [ ] Lake gear: life jackets (sized per kid), water shoes, sand toys
- [ ] Rainy-afternoon backup: games, cards, movies downloaded offline
- [ ] Snacks for the drive + meltdown insurance

### Household / kitchen

- [ ] Big grocery haul — **shop before the hill**
- [ ] Coffee + a good grinder/maker if the house one is weak
- [ ] Cooler for the lake / drive
- [ ] Trash bags, dish soap, paper towels (9 people burns through it)
- [ ] Travel mugs / water bottles

### Health / safety

- [ ] Sunscreen + bug spray (lakeside = mosquitoes at dusk)
- [ ] First-aid kit + meds — nearest real pharmacy is McCall
- [ ] **Eye masks** — long June daylight, no blinds reported
- [ ] Headlamp / flashlight

## §4 — THE RIG (don't-forget = trip-killers)

The point of the trip is to come home with recordings. Full chain in `gear.md`; the
ones that end the trip if forgotten:

- [ ] **Backup drive(s)** — record to local disk; Wi-Fi is not a backup plan
- [ ] Chargers — phones, laptop, interface power, camera
- [ ] Audio interface + enough cables (XLR ×6+, ¼" ×6+, USB/Thunderbolt)
- [ ] Headphones ×2–3 + a splitter, for tracking
- [ ] **Clip lights / a lamp** — no blinds in the loft; for night sessions
- [ ] Offline copy of the smartlock code + host contact
- [ ] Camera — this is a band-content trip too (EPK / socials)

> Rental rules: keep it quiet-friendly. Loud stuff during daytime lake hours; DI / amp-sims and reamp later once the kids are down.

## §5 — TRUCK PREP (the DEVO Suburban)

Before the loaded dusk run up ID-55:

- [x] **Oil changed**
- [ ] **Passenger-side light** — bulb run today to rule it out; if the whole side's dark it's likely a ground, not the bulb
- [ ] Quick once-over: tire pressure (incl. spare), coolant, wipers / washer fluid, brakes
- [ ] **Gas up before the hill** — stations thin out north of Boise
