## Extended bio (press kit / DSP profiles, not Bandcamp's bio field)

OWNER/OPERATORS is the post-punk, metamodern project of Boise songwriter and
multi-instrumentalist Eric Eaglstun — analog grit wired to digital ghosts,
sincerity and static sharing the same channel.

The debut, _LOSS LEADER LP_, is ten tracks built in the margins: late nights
between software commits, counseling sessions, and desert flashbacks.
Bass-and-drum skeletons, glitch-bitten synths, guitars that swing from neon edge
to broken-in warmth. It's a record about fatherhood, burnout, identity, and the
stubborn refusal to disappear into the noise — sincerity sliding into irony and
back again, never winking. The first single, _Echoes and Static_, leads the way.

The songs are the surface. Underneath runs a quiet constellation of sites,
characters, and digital artifacts — a parallel performance for anyone who goes
looking. You don't need it to hear the record. It's there if you do.

_Make something honest before you make something good._

Boise, ID.
